About Virat Kohli


Virat Kohli(born 5 November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and the former all-format captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batter and occasional right-arm medium-pace bowler. Virat Kohli is one of the most successful and decorated cricketers in history, holding multiple world records, leading India to historic Test and white-ball triumphs, and anchoring Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) to the IPL championship.

Achievements


  • ICC World Cups: Won the 2011 ODI World Cup and the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup.
  • ICC Champions Trophy: Champion in 2013 and 2025.
  • IPL Title: Captained the Royal Challengers Bengaluru to their inaugural Indian Premier League championship.
  • Youth Level: Captained the Indian U19 team to victory in the 2008 U19 World Cup
